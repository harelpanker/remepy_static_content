Christine Ferguson, PhD, RD

15 min Makes 1 Serving

What You'll Need

  • Cooking spray
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ cup chopped mushrooms
  • Optional: salsa and ground turmeric

Instructions

  1. Chop vegetables.
  2. Add cooking spray to a medium pan on medium heat.
  3. Sauté onions for 1 minute, then add the bell pepper and mushrooms. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Scramble eggs in a bowl and pour over vegetables. Sprinkle with ground turmeric.
  5. Continue to stir together for another 3 minutes, or until eggs are thoroughly cooked.
  6. Serve on top of or alongside toast, topped with salsa.

Tip

Feel free to omit, swap, or add any vegetables and herbs as desired

Quick breakfast meal and way to pack in vegetables at the start of your day!

High In: protein, unsaturated fat, vitamin B12, vitamin A, vitamin C, and selenium

Low In: added sugar, sodium
