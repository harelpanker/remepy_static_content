Christine Ferguson, PhD, RD

5 min Makes 1 Serving

What You'll Need

  • 1 medium banana
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened peanut butter
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 2 tablespoons ground flaxseed
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 5.3 ounces plain nonfat Greek yogurt (individual container)
  • 4 fluid ounces 100% orange juice

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients and pulse until smooth.
  2. Add extra orange juice or water as needed if too thick.
  3. Serve immediately.
