10 min Makes 1 Serving

What You'll Need

  • 1 veggie burger patty
  • 2 romaine lettuce leaves
  • ¼ cup sliced red onion
  • ¼ cup sliced avocado

Note: Nutrients calculated using a store-bought black bean veggie burger

Instructions

  1. Cook veggie patty according to package directions.
  2. Layer red onion slices, and avocado slices on the veggie burger patty.
  3. Place the prepared patty between two lettuce leaves (as you would with a hamburger bun).

Tip

Ideas for nutritious side items: air-fried or baked sweet potato fries, fruit salad, tomato basil soup.
