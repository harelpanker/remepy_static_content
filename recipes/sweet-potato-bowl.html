Christine Ferguson, PhD, RD

20 min Prep Time 40 min Total Time Makes 2 Servings

What You'll Need

  • 1 cup sweet potatoes (1-2 medium potatoes)
  • ½ cup green bell pepper
  • ½ cup red bell pepper
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 3 cups chopped broccoli
  • 1 cup chickpeas
  • 2½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced avocado
  • Optional seasonings:
    • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F and line a large sheet pan with parchment paper.
  2. Prep the veggies:
    • Pierce the sweet potato with a fork all over and place in the microwave for 5 minutes, flipping on the other side after 2.5 minutes. Remove and let cool for a minute. Dice into small pieces (important so they cook in time with everything else)
    • Remove the stems and seeds and coarsely chop the peppers
    • Cut the beans in half
    • Coarsely chop the broccoli
  3. Place all the veggies on the sheet pan and drizzle with olive oil and seasonings. Spread everything into one even layer.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes, flip/stir veggies, and bake for another 10-20 minutes (depends on size you have cut your veggies and desired softness level).
  5. Meanwhile, slice avocado and rinse/drain chickpeas.
  6. Remove veggies from oven, add to bowl and top with sliced avocados and chickpeas.
